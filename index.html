Iroko's protection
Advance repayable by company — not personally by partners. Longstop date 18 months. No personal liability unless fraud.
AlphaGen's protection
Not repayable if failure caused by Iroko withdrawing a transaction or failing to progress an introduced investor in good faith within 60 days.
Pipeline transfer clause
If partnership dissolves, cooperation required for 30–60 day handover. Originating partner retains tail fee — 50% within 12 months of exit, 25% in months 12–24.
Named pipeline protection
Any SA investor formally registered at signing is exclusively AlphaGen's for the tail period. Iroko cannot approach them directly. Non-negotiable.
